X (Twitter)

wasteof.money

8/10. Can't wait for wasteof 4 to come out.


Threads

Threads is like Twitter but Mark Zuckerberg.

Good community sometimes. They also don't push political content, so that's nice.

7/10


Bluesky

Bluesky is like Twitter if it was dead.

Discovery is very bad, and there's just not a lot going on.

5/10


Blaze

Blaze is a great platform if you like being tracked.

I'm really hoping this platform goes somewhere because right now it is a mess.

1/10


Counter.Social

This is basically Twitter if it was trying to shove as much news as possible into your face.

It's also suspiciously similar to Mastodon.

3/10. Not recommended.


GameJolt

This one is a weird one.

It's like Scratch, TikTok, and Twitter combined.

2/10. Why would you use this?


Meower

Now we're getting somewhere

6/10


Mastodon

The elephant in the room.

It's like Bluesky but more confusing.

3/10


Conclusion

There's no such thing as a good Twitter alternative.
